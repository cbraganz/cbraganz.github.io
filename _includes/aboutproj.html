About The Project

This website offers free outreach resources to teach soft robotics using rice paper—a rapid, low-cost, and eco-friendly fabrication method. Traditional prototyping often relies on silicone elastomers, which pose environmental challenges due to their use of non-renewable materials, energy-intensive manufacturing, and disposal issues. Rice paper presents a sustainable alternative, ideal for short-term applications like outreach and education. Here, you'll find: Guidance on how to fabricate soft actuators using rice paper Information on its benefits and end-of-life compostability Ready-to-use outreach resources, including workshop slides and materials lists Made from natural, plant-based ingredients, these materials offer a renewable, biodegradable option compared to synthetic silicones—helping reduce waste and environmental impact while making robotics accessible and fun.
