Linear Actuator Tutorial

Watch a tutorial on how to create a linear actuator below!

Corresponding steps are detailed below:

a) Spray or lightly distribute vegetable oil on the rod using hands or a non-absorbent cloth.
b) Take a container wide and deep enough to submerge the rice paper sheet.
c) Fill the container with water.
d) Submerge the rice paper in water for 10 seconds, being careful not to break or rip it.
e) Remove the rice paper and place it on a clean surface.
f) Leave the rice paper to sit and soften for 1 minute.
g) Place the oiled rod at the top end of the rice paper.
h) Roll the rod up in the rice paper tightly, being careful not to rip it.
i) Ensure the bottom of the rice paper is sealed.
j) Slide hands toward the center, squeezing the rice paper together to create folds.
k) Hold in the crumpled position for 15 seconds.
l) Slide the rice paper off the rod.
m) Insert a pump (e.g., tubing connected to a syringe) into one opening.
n) Clip the ends to seal. If unavailable, pinch and tie with string or a zip-tie.
o) Inflate and test the actuator.
